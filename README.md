# 🍽️ Zomato Restaurant Analytics Dashboard

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** that analyzes restaurant data from **Zomato** to uncover customer preferences, restaurant performance, pricing trends, cuisine popularity, and geographical distribution.

The dashboard enables users to explore restaurant insights through dynamic visualizations, helping businesses understand market trends and make informed decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze restaurant distribution across different cities.
- Identify the most popular cuisines.
- Understand customer rating patterns.
- Compare restaurant pricing levels.
- Explore online delivery and table booking trends.
- Generate actionable business insights using interactive visualizations.

---

- Total Restaurants

- Online Delivery %
- Table Booking %
- Number of Cuisines
- Restaurant Distribution

---

# 📈 Key Insights

- Restaurants are concentrated in major metropolitan cities.
- A small number of cuisines dominate the restaurant market.
- Higher-rated restaurants generally provide online delivery.
- Premium restaurants show better average customer ratings.
- Price range significantly influences customer preferences.
- Online delivery has become a major competitive advantage.
